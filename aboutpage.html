INTRODUCTION



i! I am a 20 y/o designer, art enthusiast and student based out of Mumbai. I am dedicated and hard-working towards my work and always look forward to learning from people and enthusiasts I meet. I have previously freelanced with start-ups creating their brand identity which are registered in government portals. When it comes to inspiration, I like to take a deep breath and look around, spend time with the nature and pen down every idea that strikes me. I dream of going out of the regular pen-paper and wish to use my creativity through animation. The very idea of bringing a bunch of drawings to life absolutely excites me. Having captured a good design sense over the year, I hope you enjoy my creations and consider my presence.
